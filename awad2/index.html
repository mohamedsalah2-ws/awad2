About

Awad Al Darmaki is a prominent Emirati poet and writer, celebrated for his profound contributions to contemporary Arabic literature. Known for his thought-provoking articles and inspiring lectures, he has touched countless hearts through his poetry evenings and literary works. His writing explores themes of heritage, identity, and human experience, bridging traditional Arabic literary forms with modern sensibilities.

"Poetry is the voice of the soul, speaking truths that transcend time and place."

Through his eloquent expression and deep understanding of human nature, Al Darmaki continues to influence and inspire readers across the Arab world and beyond. His work serves as a bridge between generations, preserving cultural heritage while addressing contemporary themes that resonate with modern audiences.
